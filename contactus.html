FAQs

Q. Do I need to have an account to shop with you?
A. Order can be placed without making an account. But, if you set up an account, 
then you do not have to enter your details every time you shop with us.

Q. How will I retrieve my password if I forget it?
A. You can click on ‘Forgot Password’ available at the sign-in page. 
Enter your email address and click on ‘Submit’. An email will be sent to you to recover your password.

Q. What payment method can I use?
A. We offer the following payment methods:
Cash on delivery
Credit card 
Q. What shipping methods are available?
A. We use TCS for shipping in Pakistan.

Q. Is my personal data kept secured?
A. Yes, your data is secure with us.

Q. Why should I know about cookies?
A. Cookies are small text files. We use cookies to collect information about use of our online services.

Q. Will I receive an email every time I place an order with you?
A. After confirmation of the order from the website, you will receive an email with order number and detail.
